## Active target

Architectural firms and Interior Design Studios. High-ticket projects (₹8–50L+),
high-consideration buyers, research-heavy, communicate over WhatsApp + IG.

## Top 3 pains (ranked by revenue impact × AI fit)

| # | Pain | What it costs them | AI fix | Hook line |
|---|------|--------------------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | **Slow lead response** | Inquiries sit 24–72 hrs; prospect already talked to 2 competitors; first responder wins | Live Lead Engagement (WhatsApp/SMS/Social) — replies <60s, qualifies, books | "The firm that replies first usually gets the project." |
| 2 | **Follow-up black hole** | 40–60% of warm quotes go cold; no systematic nurture after the proposal | Lead Reactivation (SMS) + Day 2/5/10/21 sequences | "You've done the hard part — they just need 3 more touches." |
| 3 | **Senior time drain** | Principals field basic "how much?" questions; 3–5 hrs/week lost to pre-qual | Website Widget + AI pre-qualification | "Your principal's time is worth ₹5,000/hr. The bot handles the rest." |

## Product ranking for this industry

1. **Live Lead Engagement** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Pain #1. Highest revenue impact.
2. **Lead Reactivation (SMS)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Pain #2. Revenue with zero ad spend.
3. **Website Widget** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Pain #3. Strong conversion tool.
4. **Google Review Agent** ⭐⭐⭐ — Trust multiplier / local SEO. Slower revenue.

## ROI anchors (use in pitches)

- <60s lead response vs 48hr manual
- 15–25% reactivation rate on dormant quote list
- 3–5 hrs/week senior time reclaimed
- 1–2 recovered projects/quarter = ₹8–50L incremental revenue

## Outreach customization rules (all platforms: LinkedIn, WhatsApp, email, IG)

1. Open with ONE of the 3 pains — never a generic "AI automation" opener.
2. Map to ONE specific product fix (don't list the menu).
3. Anchor ROI in ₹, not just %.
4. Stay stage-aware: Setu is pre-revenue, proof-led warm outreach (see CLAUDE.md doctrine).
