# Workflow: ICP Definition (Setu — Bridge 01)

**Objective:** Single source of truth for WHO Setu targets and the offer funnel. Every sourcing,
personalization, and qualification decision references this file.

## ICP — Bridge 01
India's **interior-design studios, architecture firms, and small-to-mid construction companies**.
- Size: 3–50 people · Age: 3–15 years · **Founder-led**
- Ops today: WhatsApp + Excel + email; no automation team
- **Beachhead: interior-design studios first** (warm leads + Setu's already-built `buildbridge`
  tools + Instagram fit). Architecture second, construction later.

## Pain (in their words)
"Quotes copied between spreadsheets." · "Vendors chased three times." · "Reports cobbled together."
· "Site photos → client updates by hand." · Founder is the coordination bottleneck across functions.

## Offer funnel (ONE funnel + a fence — not two free offers)
1. **Free diagnosis** — Gemini Gem "where AI helps your design business" + a short scoping conversation.
   (Setu value: *Map before build*.)
2. **Free first build** — ONE `buildbridge` output matched to their pain (mood board / activity overlay /
   material-lighting-BOQ sheet / FF&E sheet). (Setu value: *Ship, don't slide*.)
3. **Loom walkthrough** of the deliverable (Setu voice). Proof.
4. **Fence** — delivered free in exchange for: **testimonial + case-study permission + 1 referral**.
   Anchor value: "normally ₹X — free for my first founding studios."
5. **Paid** — once proof exists / capacity fills. **Cap: 7 free founding clients.**

## Channels × geography
- **Warm, inside India (now):** LinkedIn + WhatsApp + Instagram + Loom.
- **Cold (Phase 2):** NRIs + any English-speaking design market worldwide.

## Brand rules (hard)
- No proof claims before real engagements exist.
- Voice: quiet, plainspoken, ROI-first. Forbidden words enforced by `tools/setu_voice.py`.
- "Built, not advised" — lead with a real built thing, not advice.

## Qualify (lightweight, warm phase)
A lead is worth the free build if: founder-led · visible manual pain · reachable on LinkedIn/WhatsApp/IG ·
interior (beachhead) or strong adjacent fit. Else → Nurture.
