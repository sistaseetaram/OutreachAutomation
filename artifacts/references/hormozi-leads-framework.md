## The 5 Lead Sources

Every business gets leads from some combination of these 5 sources:

| Source | Effort | Conversion | Speed |
|---|---|---|---|
| Warm Outreach | Low | Highest | Fast |
| Cold Outreach | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Content | High (upfront) | Low initially | Slow |
| Paid Ads | Medium | Varies | Fast |
| Affiliates | Low (after setup) | High | Fast |

For AI agencies starting out: **Cold Outreach + Warm Outreach** are the fastest paths to first clients.

## Cold Outreach System

### Step 1: Avatar Definition
- Who specifically are you targeting?
- Industry, company size, title, pain points
- Avoid "anyone who could use AI" — too broad

### Step 2: List Building
- Sources: LinkedIn, Apollo.io, Hunter.io, Crunchbase
- Build list of 100+ initial targets in specific niche
- Verify emails before sending

### Step 3: Segmentation
- Group prospects by characteristics (industry, size, pain)
- Tailor messaging to each segment
- Never send same generic message to all

### Step 4: Cold Email/DM Structure
- **Subject**: curiosity-based or specific benefit
- **Opening**: relevant observation or problem statement (personalized)
- **Body**: one specific pain they likely have + how you solve it
- **CTA**: single clear ask — brief call or simple reply, never "buy now"
- **Length**: shorter is better. Under 150 words for email.

### Step 5: Follow-Up Sequence (3 touches minimum)
- Touch 1: Initial outreach
- Touch 2 (Day 3–5): Different angle or proof point
- Touch 3 (Day 7–10): Scarcity/final follow-up or pivot to different channel
- After 3: Move to next prospect

### Step 6: Multi-Channel
- If no reply to email → try LinkedIn DM
- If no reply to DM → try email
- Don't spam same channel repeatedly

## Warm Outreach System

- Higher close rate than cold — use first
- Sources: past contacts, referrals, LinkedIn connections
- Approach: value first, ask second
- Template: "Hey [name], I'm building an AI audit business. I know [specific thing about them]. Would you be open to a 20-min call to see if there's a fit?"

---

## Reply Rate Benchmarks

Generic cold email: 2–5% reply rate
Personalized cold email: 10–20% reply rate
Highly targeted + personalized: 20–35% reply rate

If below 5%: fix the offer or the targeting before sending more

---

## Key Principle

Outreach is a numbers + quality game:
- Send 10-20 per day minimum for consistent pipeline
- Track: open rate, reply rate, meeting rate, close rate
- Optimize the weakest link first
- Never stop — pipeline dries up 30 days after you stop outreaching

## Cold Outreach for AI Agency

**Subject lines that work:**
- "Quick question about [Company]'s ops"
- "Found something re: [specific pain] at [Company]"
- "[First name] — saw you're hiring [role], had a thought"

**Opening hooks:**
- "I saw [Company] is scaling fast based on [specific signal] — usually means [specific operational pain]"
- "Your hiring for [role] caught my attention — that usually means [pain point]"
- "Most [industry] companies I talk to waste [X hours/week] on [specific task]"

**CTA:**
- "Would a 15-min call this week make sense?"
- "Open to a quick chat on Thursday or Friday?"
- "Worth a 10-min call?"
