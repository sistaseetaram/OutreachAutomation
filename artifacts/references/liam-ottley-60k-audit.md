## Process: 4-Week Embedded Audit

**Week 1–2: Interview Phase**
- Interview 20–30 staff members across all levels
- Map every business process end-to-end
- Cover all 3 engines: Acquisition, Delivery, Support
- Use structured interview templates (stakeholder + end-user)

**Week 3: Analysis Phase**
- Translate interview notes into Ops Canvas (Excalidraw process maps)
- Build AI Opportunity Matrix (Quick Wins vs. Big Swings)
- Validate with key stakeholders
- Build ROI calculations for top opportunities

**Week 4: Presentation Phase**
- Deliver 5-slide executive deck
- Present Quick Wins with implementation timeline
- Present Big Swings roadmap (Phase 2 work)
- Show Money Slide — annual cost savings + revenue uplift

## Process Maps as Core Deliverable

The audit's most tangible deliverable is **visual process maps** in Excalidraw (or similar):
- Flowcharts showing every step in each business engine
- Color-coded friction points (time sinks, quality risks)
- Shows client where they are TODAY
- Makes invisible inefficiencies visible
- Clients have often never seen their own processes drawn out

**Why this commands premium pricing:**
- Clients pay for clarity, not just recommendations
- The map becomes the source of truth for all future AI work
- It's a deliverable they can share internally
- It positions you as a strategic partner, not a vendor
