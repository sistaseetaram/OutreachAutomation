# Current Outreach Strategy

Date: 2026-06-07
Channel: LinkedIn Sales Navigator
ICP: Architecture studio founders and principals in Bangalore and Hyderabad

## Daily Operating Target

- Send 20 connection requests per day unless LinkedIn Business limits or account-health signals require reducing volume.
- Prioritize architecture founders, principals, partners, and studio owners over junior architects.
- Source from Sales Navigator, not regular LinkedIn search.

## Campaign Split

- 50% `variant:soft-touch`
  - No direct pitch in the first touch.
  - Ask for perspective, guidance, or a 15-minute scoping conversation around architecture workflow automation.
- 50% `variant:offer-campaign`
  - Proof-led message tied to the Nine Bricks Studio V1/fix content.
  - Position as building workflow automations for two more architecture firms this month.

## Channel Rules

- Use `channel:connection` for normal Sales Navigator connection requests and follow-up DMs.
- Reserve `channel:inmail` for highly qualified founders only.
- InMail credits are limited; spend only when the profile is a strong fit and a reply would likely be valuable.

- Review performance after 100 sends.
- Keep and refine the strategy if replies are trending toward 20-30% or better.
