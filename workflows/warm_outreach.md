# Workflow: Warm Outreach (founding clients)

**Objective:** Convert your ~5 existing warm contacts (interior/architecture studios) into free
founding-client builds → case studies. This is Phase 1's fastest path to proof.

## Who
People you already know / soft intros, in interior design (beachhead) or adjacent. Start here —
warm converts highest (Hormozi). First case study = Nine Bricks Studio (your sister, Manjusha).

## Steps (per warm lead)
1. **Research** — `company_researcher.py`; confirm their #1 manual pain.
2. **First touch** — `personalization_engine.py --channel whatsapp|linkedin_dm --stage first_touch`.
   Warm, peer, one observation. No pitch.
3. **Free diagnosis** — share the Gemini Gem + a short scoping chat: map ONE workflow with ROI.
4. **Free build** — deliver ONE `buildbridge` output for that workflow (see `free_build_delivery.md`).
5. **Loom** — script with `loom-outreach` skill (or `loom-walkthrough-recorder` for the deliverable demo).
6. **Fence** — ask for: testimonial + case-study permission + 1 referral. Anchor: "normally ₹X".
7. **Track** — `lead_tracker.py --advance` through Replied → Diagnosis Booked → Free Build → Delivered →
   Testimonial/Referral. Artifacts → Drive (Tier A → Tier B on booking).

## Tone
Quiet, honest, conservative on ROI (measure on one real project — don't inflate). Respect the relationship.

## Cap
Max 7 free founding clients. Beyond that → start charging.
